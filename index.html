You matter to God. And to us.

Welcome to a church where everyone matters, everyone belongs, and everyone has something important to contribute. At Willow Creek, you'll experience practical, down-to-earth teaching, powerful music, and real-life stories you can relate to. You'll sense our commitment to extending Christ's compassion to our broken world, both locally and globally. And no matter where you are in your spiritual journey, you'll find plenty of opportunities to grow, get connected, and give back to your community.

Choose from eight locations in the Chicago area to find a Willow near you! Meet people eager to encounter the love of Christ in deeper ways and take steps to grow—in their relationships with God and with one another.
